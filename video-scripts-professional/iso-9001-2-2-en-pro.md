Who are Stakeholders?

(1:00 - 5:00) Stakeholders are individuals or organizations affected by the organization or affecting it.

Individuals or organizations affected Individuals or organizations affecting May be internal or external May be direct or indirect of Understanding Stakeholders: Helps identify needs Helps identify expectations Helps improve relationships Helps improve performance Stakeholders include customers Stakeholders include employees Stakeholders include suppliers Stakeholders include the community Types of Stakeholders (5:00 - 10:00) There are several types of stakeholders.

Internal Stakeholders: Employees Top management Shareholders Managers External Stakeholders: Customers Suppliers Regulators Community Direct Stakeholders: Direct customers Direct employees Direct suppliers Indirect Stakeholders: Community Government Non-governmental organizations Competitors Stakeholder Needs and Expectations (10:00 - 14:00) Each stakeholder has different needs and expectations.

Customer Needs: Product quality Reasonable price Excellent service Product availability Employee Needs: Safe work environment Fair compensation Development opportunities Work appreciation Supplier Needs: On-time payment Long-term relationships Clear requirements Mutual collaboration Regulator Needs: Regulatory compliance Regular reports Transparency Product quality Community Needs: Environmental protection Social responsibility Safe products Community contribution Stakeholder Management (14:00 - 18:00) Stakeholder management is essential for success.

Quality policy is the foundation upon which the quality management system is built.

What is Quality Policy?

(1:00 - 5:00) Quality policy is a statement from top management clarifying its commitment to quality.

Formal statement from top management Clarifies organization's commitment to quality Defines main objectives Provides framework for detailed objectives of Quality Policy: Provides clear direction Provides framework for decisions Provides basis for objectives Provides basis for improvement Quality policy must be appropriate Quality policy must be clear Quality policy must be measurable Quality policy must be achievable Quality Policy Elements (5:00 - 10:00) Quality policy must include several essential elements.

Main Elements: Commitment to Quality: Commitment to quality and compliance Commitment to international standards Commitment to continuous improvement Commitment to customer satisfaction Main Objectives: Improve product quality Improve customer satisfaction Improve efficiency Improve performance Focus on Customers: Understand customer needs Meet customer expectations Exceed customer expectations Measure customer satisfaction Continuous Improvement: Commitment to continuous improvement Encourage innovation Encourage improvement Encourage learning Alignment: Alignment with organization policy Alignment with organization strategy Alignment with organization values Alignment with organization culture Developing Quality Policy (10:00 - 13:00) Developing quality policy requires following specific procedures.

Steps: Evaluate Current Situation: Evaluate current quality policy Evaluate organization needs Evaluate customer needs Evaluate regulatory requirements Develop Draft: Develop policy draft Review draft with top management Review draft with employees Review draft with stakeholders Approval: Approval from top management Approval from quality manager Documentation Signature Communication: Communicate with all employees Communicate with stakeholders Communicate with customers Communicate with suppliers Reviewing and Updating Quality Policy (13:00 - 15:00) Quality policy must be reviewed and updated regularly.

When to Review Policy?

During major changes During strategy changes During market changes During organizational changes Periodically (annually or every two years) How to Review Policy?

What is Cleaning Validation?

(1:00 - 5:00) Cleaning Validation is the process of demonstrating that cleaning procedures are effective in removing contaminants.

Demonstrating that cleaning is effective Demonstrating that contaminants are completely removed Demonstrating that no contaminant residues remain Demonstrating that cleaning is consistent of Cleaning Validation: Prevents cross-contamination Ensures product quality Ensures patient safety Essential for regulatory compliance Cleaning Validation is essential for equipment used for multiple products Cleaning Validation must be performed before use Cleaning Validation must be documented Cleaning Validation Principles (5:00 - 9:00) Cleaning Validation must follow specific principles.

Identification: Identify target contaminants Selection: Select appropriate cleaning method Verification: Verify cleaning effectiveness Documentation: Complete documentation Monitoring: Continuous monitoring Accurate contaminant identification is essential Selecting appropriate method is essential Verifying effectiveness is essential Complete documentation is essential Continuous monitoring is essential Cleaning Validation Stages (9:00 - 15:00) Cleaning Validation includes several organized stages.

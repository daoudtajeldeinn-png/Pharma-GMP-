What is Competence?

(1:00 - 5:00) Competence is the ability to execute tasks correctly and effectively.

Required knowledge Required skills Required experience Ability to apply of Competence: Ensures work quality Reduces errors Increases efficiency Increases effectiveness Competence must be appropriate for the role Competence must be current Competence must be evaluated Competence must be documented Types of Competence (5:00 - 10:00) There are several types of competence that must be developed.

Technical Competence: Technical knowledge Technical skills Technical experience Technical application ability Management Competence: Management knowledge Management skills Management experience Management ability Operational Competence: Operational knowledge Operational skills Operational experience Operational ability Quality Competence: Quality knowledge Quality skills Quality experience Quality management ability Organizational Competence: Organizational knowledge Organizational skills Organizational experience Organizational ability Developing Competence (10:00 - 14:00) Developing competence requires following specific procedures.

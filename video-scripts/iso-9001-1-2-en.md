# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 1.2
## Quality Management Principles
**Duration:** 15 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss Quality Management Principles. These principles are the foundation upon which the quality management system is built.

### The Seven Quality Management Principles (1:00 - 8:00)
**Speaker:** ISO 9001 is based on seven fundamental quality management principles.

**Principle 1: Customer Focus**
- Customer is the central focus
- Understanding customer needs
- Meeting customer expectations
- Exceeding customer expectations

**Principle 2: Leadership**
- Top management has a vital role
- Leadership creates a positive work environment
- Leadership sets goals and direction
- Leadership encourages continuous improvement

**Principle 3: Engagement of People**
- Employees are the most important assets
- Employee participation in decisions
- Empowering employees
- Developing employee skills

**Principle 4: Process Approach**
- Understanding processes as an integrated system
- Managing processes effectively
- Continuous process improvement
- Focus on results

**Principle 5: Improvement**
- Continuous improvement is essential
- Continuously improving performance
- Continuously improving processes
- Continuously improving products

**Principle 6: Evidence-based Decision Making**
- Making decisions based on data
- Analyzing data accurately
- Using reliable information
- Avoiding arbitrary decisions

**Principle 7: Relationship Management**
- Managing relationships with suppliers
- Managing relationships with partners
- Managing relationships with customers
- Managing relationships with the community

**Speaker:** Let's see how these principles are applied in practical practice.

**Example 1: Customer Focus:**

**Example 5: Improvement:**

**Example 7: Relationship Management:**
- Select appropriate suppliers
- Build long-term relationships
- Continuous communication

### Benefits of Applying Principles (12:00 - 15:00)
**Speaker:** Applying these principles provides significant benefits to the organization.

**Main Benefits:**
1. **Improved Customer Satisfaction:** Customer focus increases satisfaction
2. **Improved Performance:** Leadership and engagement improve performance
3. **Improved Efficiency:** Process approach increases efficiency
4. **Improved Quality:** Continuous improvement improves quality
5. **Improved Decisions:** Evidence-based decisions are better
6. **Improved Relationships:** Relationship management creates strong partnerships

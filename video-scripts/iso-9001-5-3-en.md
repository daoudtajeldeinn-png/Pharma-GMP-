# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 5.3
## Awareness
**Duration:** 15 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss awareness in the quality management system. Awareness is essential for the success of the quality management system.

### What is Awareness? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Awareness is employees' understanding of the quality management system and their role in it.

**Definition:**
- Understanding quality policy
- Understanding quality objectives
- Understanding responsibilities
- Understanding impact on quality

**Importance of Awareness:**
- Ensures active participation
- Ensures correct execution
- Ensures continuous improvement
- Ensures system success

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Awareness must be comprehensive
- Awareness must be continuous
- Awareness must be evaluated
- Awareness must be improved

### Awareness Elements (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** Awareness includes several essential elements.

**Main Elements:**

**1. Policy Awareness:**
- Understanding quality policy
- Understanding management commitment
- Understanding customer focus
- Understanding commitment to improvement

**2. Objective Awareness:**
- Understanding quality objectives
- Understanding how to contribute to achieving objectives
- Understanding how performance is measured
- Understanding importance of achieving objectives

**3. Responsibility Awareness:**
- Understanding role and responsibilities
- Understanding how role affects quality
- Understanding importance of role
- Understanding accountability

**4. Risk Awareness:**
- Understanding potential risks
- Understanding how risks affect quality
- Understanding how risks are managed
- Understanding role in risk management

**5. Opportunity Awareness:**
- Understanding potential opportunities
- Understanding how opportunities affect quality
- Understanding how opportunities are exploited
- Understanding role in exploiting opportunities

### Raising Awareness (10:00 - 13:00)
**Speaker:** Raising awareness requires following specific procedures.

**Procedures:**

**1. Continuous Communication:**
- Communicate about policy
- Communicate about objectives
- Communicate about changes
- Communicate about performance

**2. Continuous Training:**
- Training on policy
- Training on objectives
- Training on procedures
- Training on skills

**3. Participation:**
- Employee participation in decisions
- Employee participation in improvement
- Employee participation in evaluation
- Employee participation in planning

**4. Reward:**
- Reward good performance
- Reward improvement
- Reward innovation
- Reward participation

### Evaluating Awareness (13:00 - 15:00)
**Speaker:** Evaluating awareness is essential to ensure effectiveness.

**Evaluation Methods:**

**1. Surveys:**
- Surveys to measure awareness
- Surveys to measure understanding
- Surveys to measure satisfaction
- Surveys to measure participation

**2. Interviews:**
- Individual interviews
- Group interviews
- Interviews with supervisors
- Interviews with employees

**3. Monitoring:**
- Monitor behavior
- Monitor performance
- Monitor participation
- Monitor improvement

**4. Tests:**
- Tests on policy
- Tests on objectives
- Tests on procedures
- Tests on skills

### Summary (15:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What is awareness
- Awareness elements
- Raising awareness
- Evaluating awareness

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Awareness is essential for success
- Awareness includes several elements
- Raising awareness requires procedures
- Evaluating awareness is essential

This completes the ISO 9001 course. Thank you for following.

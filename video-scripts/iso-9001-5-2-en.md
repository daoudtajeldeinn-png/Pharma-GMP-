# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 5.2
## Competence
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss competence in the quality management system. Competence is essential for the success of the quality management system.

### What is Competence? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Competence is the ability to execute tasks correctly and effectively.

**Definition:**
- Required knowledge
- Required skills
- Required experience
- Ability to apply

**Importance of Competence:**
- Ensures work quality
- Reduces errors
- Increases efficiency
- Increases effectiveness

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Competence must be appropriate for the role
- Competence must be current
- Competence must be evaluated
- Competence must be documented

### Types of Competence (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** There are several types of competence that must be developed.

**Main Types:**

**1. Technical Competence:**
- Technical knowledge
- Technical skills
- Technical experience
- Technical application ability

**2. Management Competence:**
- Management knowledge
- Management skills
- Management experience
- Management ability

**3. Operational Competence:**
- Operational knowledge
- Operational skills
- Operational experience
- Operational ability

**4. Quality Competence:**
- Quality knowledge
- Quality skills
- Quality experience
- Quality management ability

**5. Organizational Competence:**
- Organizational knowledge
- Organizational skills
- Organizational experience
- Organizational ability

### Developing Competence (10:00 - 14:00)
**Speaker:** Developing competence requires following specific procedures.

**Procedures:**

**1. Identify Competence Needs:**
- Identify required competence
- Identify current competence
- Identify gaps
- Identify priorities

**2. Develop Training Plan:**
- Define required training
- Define training methods
- Define timeline
- Define resources

**3. Execute Training:**
- Execute training according to plan
- Monitor training
- Evaluate training
- Document

**4. Apply Competence:**
- Apply competence in work
- Monitor application
- Evaluate application
- Improve application

**5. Evaluate Competence:**
- Evaluate competence after training
- Evaluate training effectiveness
- Identify improvement areas
- Update training plan

### Evaluating Competence (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Evaluating competence is essential to ensure effectiveness.

**Evaluation Methods:**

**1. Self-Evaluation:**
- Employee evaluates themselves
- Employee evaluates their skills
- Employee evaluates their needs
- Employee evaluates their objectives

**2. Supervisor Evaluation:**
- Supervisor evaluates employee
- Supervisor evaluates performance
- Supervisor evaluates skills
- Supervisor evaluates behavior

**3. Peer Evaluation:**
- Colleagues evaluate each other
- Colleagues evaluate collaboration
- Colleagues evaluate communication
- Colleagues evaluate teamwork

**4. Formal Evaluation:**
- Formal tests
- Formal certificates
- Formal accreditations
- Formal evaluations

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What is competence
- Types of competence
- Developing competence
- Evaluating competence

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Competence is essential for success
- There are several types of competence
- Continuous development is essential
- Continuous evaluation is essential

In the next lecture, we will discuss Awareness.

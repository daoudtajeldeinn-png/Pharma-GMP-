# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 5.1
## Support
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss support in the quality management system. Support is essential for the success of the quality management system.

### Types of Support (1:00 - 6:00)
**Speaker:** There are several types of support that must be provided.

**Main Types:**

**1. Resources:**
- Human resources
- Financial resources
- Technical resources
- Physical resources
- Infrastructure

**2. Competence:**
- Required competence
- Required training
- Required education
- Required experience
- Required skills

**3. Awareness:**
- Awareness of policy
- Awareness of objectives
- Awareness of responsibilities
- Awareness of risks
- Awareness of opportunities

**4. Documentation:**
- Required documents
- Required records
- Required information
- Required knowledge
- Required data

**5. Communication:**
- Internal communication
- External communication
- Formal communication
- Informal communication
- Continuous communication

### Providing Resources (6:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** Providing adequate resources is essential for the success of the quality management system.

**Main Resources:**

**1. Human Resources:**
- Sufficient number of employees
- Appropriate employee skills
- Appropriate employee training
- Appropriate employee experience

**2. Financial Resources:**
- Adequate budget
- Appropriate budget allocation
- Monitor spending
- Improve resource utilization

**3. Technical Resources:**
- Appropriate equipment
- Appropriate software
- Appropriate systems
- Periodic maintenance

**4. Physical Resources:**
- Appropriate raw materials
- Appropriate products
- Appropriate facilities
- Appropriate environment

**5. Infrastructure:**
- Information systems
- Communication systems
- Security systems
- Emergency systems

### Developing Competence (10:00 - 14:00)
**Speaker:** Developing competence is essential for the success of the quality management system.

**Competence Development Procedures:**

**1. Identify Competence Needs:**
- Identify required competence
- Identify current competence
- Identify gaps
- Identify priorities

**2. Develop Training Plan:**
- Define required training
- Define training methods
- Define timeline
- Define resources

**3. Execute Training:**
- Execute training according to plan
- Monitor training
- Evaluate training
- Document

**4. Evaluate Competence:**
- Evaluate competence after training
- Evaluate training effectiveness
- Identify improvement areas
- Update training plan

### Raising Awareness (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Raising awareness is essential for the success of the quality management system.

**Awareness Raising Procedures:**

**1. Continuous Communication:**
- Communicate about policy
- Communicate about objectives
- Communicate about changes
- Communicate about performance

**2. Continuous Training:**
- Training on policy
- Training on objectives
- Training on procedures
- Training on skills

**3. Participation:**
- Employee participation in decisions
- Employee participation in improvement
- Employee participation in evaluation
- Employee participation in planning

**4. Reward:**
- Reward good performance
- Reward improvement
- Reward innovation
- Reward participation

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- Types of support
- Providing resources
- Developing competence
- Raising awareness

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Support is essential for success
- Adequate resources are essential
- Competence is essential
- Awareness is essential

In the next lecture, we will discuss Competence in detail.

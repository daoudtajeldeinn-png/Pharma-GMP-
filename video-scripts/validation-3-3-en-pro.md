PQ is the third and final stage of validation and is essential to ensure that equipment performs as required under actual operating conditions.
What is PQ?
(1:00 - 5:00) PQ is the verification of equipment performance under actual operating conditions.
Performance qualification Ensure equipment performs as required Ensure consistency and stability Ensure product quality of PQ: Ensures product quality Reduces deviations Ensures patient safety Essential for commercial production PQ must be performed after OQ PQ must be executed under actual operating conditions PQ requires execution of multiple batches PQ Stages (5:00 - 10:00) PQ includes several organized stages.

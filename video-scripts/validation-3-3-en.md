# Video Script: Validation - Unit 3.3
## PQ (Performance Qualification)
**Duration:** 22 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss PQ or Performance Qualification. PQ is the third and final stage of validation and is essential to ensure that equipment performs as required under actual operating conditions.

### What is PQ? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** PQ is the verification of equipment performance under actual operating conditions.

**Definition:**
- Performance qualification
- Ensure equipment performs as required
- Ensure consistency and stability
- Ensure product quality

**Importance of PQ:**
- Ensures product quality
- Reduces deviations
- Ensures patient safety
- Essential for commercial production

**Detailed Explanation:**
- PQ must be performed after OQ
- PQ must be executed under actual operating conditions
- PQ requires execution of multiple batches

### PQ Stages (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** PQ includes several organized stages.

**Stages:**

**1. PQ Stage 1:**
- Process design and development
- Identify parameters
- Define acceptance limits
- Develop control procedures

**2. PQ Stage 2:**
- Process qualification
- Execute 3 successful batches
- Verify consistency
- Verify quality

**3. PQ Stage 3:**
- Continued process qualification
- Monitor performance
- Monitor quality
- Continuous improvement

**Detailed Explanation for Each Stage:**

**PQ Stage 1:**
- Process design and development
- Identify critical parameters
- Identify critical control points
- Develop control strategy

**PQ Stage 2:**
- Execute 3 successful batches
- Verify consistency
- Verify quality
- Verify compliance

**PQ Stage 3:**
- Continuous performance monitoring
- Continuous quality monitoring
- Continuous improvement
- Continuous maintenance

### PQ Contents (10:00 - 16:00)
**Speaker:** PQ includes several elements that must be verified.

**Main Elements:**

**1. Batch Execution:**
- Execute 3 successful batches
- Execute batches according to formula
- Monitor all parameters
- Record all data

**2. Quality Testing:**
- Test final product
- Test critical parameters
- Test critical control points
- Compare results with criteria

**3. Data Analysis:**
- Analyze batch data
- Compare batches with each other
- Verify consistency
- Verify stability

**4. Performance Evaluation:**
- Evaluate equipment performance
- Evaluate process performance
- Evaluate product quality
- Evaluate compliance

**5. Documentation:**
- Document all activities
- Prepare PQ report
- Review report
- Approve report

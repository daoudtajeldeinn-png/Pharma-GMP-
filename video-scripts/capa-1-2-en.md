## Regulatory Requirements for CAPA
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss regulatory requirements for CAPA. Understanding regulations is essential for compliance.

### FDA CAPA Guidance (1:00 - 6:00)
**Speaker:** FDA CAPA Guidance is one of the most important regulatory references for CAPA.

**Main Requirements:**
1. **Effective CAPA System:** Company must have an effective CAPA system
2. **Problem Investigation:** All problems must be investigated
3. **Corrective Actions:** Effective corrective actions must be developed
4. **Preventive Actions:** Effective preventive actions must be developed
5. **Documentation:** All CAPA activities must be documented

**Detailed Explanation:**
- FDA requires a comprehensive CAPA system
- FDA requires comprehensive investigations
- FDA requires effective actions
- FDA requires complete documentation

### ICH Q10 (6:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** ICH Q10 is an international guidance for pharmaceutical quality management system.

**Main Requirements:**
1. **Quality Management System:** Company must have an effective quality management system
2. **Risk Management:** Must include risk management
3. **Continuous Improvement:** Must have continuous improvement
4. **Management Review:** Must have periodic management review
5. **Communication:** Must have effective communication

**Detailed Explanation:**
- ICH Q10 focuses on product lifecycle
- ICH Q10 focuses on risk management
- ICH Q10 focuses on continuous improvement
- ICH Q10 focuses on management review

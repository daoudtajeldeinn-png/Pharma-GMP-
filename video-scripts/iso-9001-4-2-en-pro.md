Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 2 Risks and Opportunities Duration: 20 minutes (0:00 - 1:00) In this lecture, we will discuss risks and opportunities.
Managing risks and opportunities is essential for the success of the quality management system.
What are Risks?
(1:00 - 5:00) Risks are uncertain events that may negatively affect the organization.
Uncertain events May negatively affect objectives May negatively affect quality May negatively affect customers Examples of Risks: Equipment failure Employee errors Market changes Regulatory changes Supply chain problems Risks must be identified Risks must be evaluated Risks must be managed Risks must be monitored What are Opportunities?
(5:00 - 9:00) Opportunities are uncertain events that may positively affect the organization.
Uncertain events May positively affect objectives May positively affect quality May positively affect customers Examples of Opportunities: New technologies New markets Process improvements New partnerships Innovations Opportunities must be identified Opportunities must be evaluated Opportunities must be exploited Opportunities must be monitored Risk Management (9:00 - 14:00) Risk management is essential to prevent problems and reduce damages.
Risk Management Identify Risks: Identify all potential risks Identify risk sources Identify risk impact Identify risk probability Evaluate Risks: Evaluate probability of occurrence Evaluate severity of impact Determine risk level Determine priorities Address Risks: Develop control procedures Develop contingency plans Develop preventive procedures Develop response procedures Monitor Risks: Monitor risks continuously Monitor procedure effectiveness Monitor changes in risks Update evaluations Exploiting Opportunities (14:00 - 18:00) Exploiting opportunities is essential to improve performance and success.
Opportunity Exploitation Identify Opportunities: Identify all potential opportunities Identify opportunity sources Identify opportunity impact Identify opportunity probability Evaluate Opportunities: Evaluate probability of exploitation Evaluate severity of impact Determine opportunity level Determine priorities Exploit Opportunities: Develop exploitation plans Provide required resources Execute plans Monitor execution Monitor Opportunities: Monitor opportunities continuously Monitor exploitation effectiveness Monitor changes in opportunities Update evaluations (18:00) In this lecture, we learned: What are risks What are opportunities Risk management Exploiting opportunities Key Points to Remember: Risks and opportunities must be identified Risks and opportunities must be evaluated Risks must be managed Opportunities must be exploited In the next lecture, we will discuss Quality Objectives.

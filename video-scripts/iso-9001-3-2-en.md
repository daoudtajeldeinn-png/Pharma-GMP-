# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 3.2
## Policy
**Duration:** 15 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss the quality policy in detail. Quality policy is the foundation upon which the quality management system is built.

### What is Quality Policy? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Quality policy is a statement from top management clarifying its commitment to quality.

**Definition:**
- Formal statement from top management
- Clarifies organization's commitment to quality
- Defines main objectives
- Provides framework for detailed objectives

**Importance of Quality Policy:**
- Provides clear direction
- Provides framework for decisions
- Provides basis for objectives
- Provides basis for improvement

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Quality policy must be appropriate
- Quality policy must be clear
- Quality policy must be measurable
- Quality policy must be achievable

### Quality Policy Elements (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** Quality policy must include several essential elements.

**Main Elements:**

**1. Commitment to Quality:**
- Commitment to quality and compliance
- Commitment to international standards
- Commitment to continuous improvement
- Commitment to customer satisfaction

**2. Main Objectives:**
- Improve product quality
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Improve efficiency
- Improve performance

**3. Focus on Customers:**
- Understand customer needs
- Meet customer expectations
- Exceed customer expectations
- Measure customer satisfaction

**4. Continuous Improvement:**
- Commitment to continuous improvement
- Encourage innovation
- Encourage improvement
- Encourage learning

**5. Alignment:**
- Alignment with organization policy
- Alignment with organization strategy
- Alignment with organization values
- Alignment with organization culture

### Developing Quality Policy (10:00 - 13:00)
**Speaker:** Developing quality policy requires following specific procedures.

**Steps:**

**1. Evaluate Current Situation:**
- Evaluate current quality policy
- Evaluate organization needs
- Evaluate customer needs
- Evaluate regulatory requirements

**2. Develop Draft:**
- Develop policy draft
- Review draft with top management
- Review draft with employees
- Review draft with stakeholders

**3. Approval:**
- Approval from top management
- Approval from quality manager
- Documentation
- Signature

**4. Communication:**
- Communicate with all employees
- Communicate with stakeholders
- Communicate with customers
- Communicate with suppliers

### Reviewing and Updating Quality Policy (13:00 - 15:00)
**Speaker:** Quality policy must be reviewed and updated regularly.

**When to Review Policy?**
- During major changes
- During strategy changes
- During market changes
- During organizational changes
- Periodically (annually or every two years)

**How to Review Policy?**
- Evaluate effectiveness
- Evaluate appropriateness
- Evaluate performance
- Identify improvement areas
- Update policy when necessary

### Summary (15:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What is quality policy
- Quality policy elements
- Developing quality policy
- Reviewing and updating quality policy

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Quality policy is essential
- Quality policy must be clear
- Quality policy must be measurable
- Continuous review is essential

In the next lecture, we will discuss Roles and Responsibilities in detail.

# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 4.2
## Risks and Opportunities
**Duration:** 20 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss risks and opportunities. Managing risks and opportunities is essential for the success of the quality management system.

### What are Risks? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Risks are uncertain events that may negatively affect the organization.

**Definition:**
- Uncertain events
- May negatively affect objectives
- May negatively affect quality
- May negatively affect customers

**Examples of Risks:**
- Equipment failure
- Employee errors
- Market changes
- Regulatory changes
- Supply chain problems

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Risks must be identified
- Risks must be evaluated
- Risks must be managed
- Risks must be monitored

### What are Opportunities? (5:00 - 9:00)
**Speaker:** Opportunities are uncertain events that may positively affect the organization.

**Definition:**
- Uncertain events
- May positively affect objectives
- May positively affect quality
- May positively affect customers

**Examples of Opportunities:**
- New technologies
- New markets
- Process improvements
- New partnerships
- Innovations

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Opportunities must be identified
- Opportunities must be evaluated
- Opportunities must be exploited
- Opportunities must be monitored

### Risk Management (9:00 - 14:00)
**Speaker:** Risk management is essential to prevent problems and reduce damages.

**Risk Management Procedures:**

**1. Identify Risks:**
- Identify all potential risks
- Identify risk sources
- Identify risk impact
- Identify risk probability

**2. Evaluate Risks:**
- Evaluate probability of occurrence
- Evaluate severity of impact
- Determine risk level
- Determine priorities

**3. Address Risks:**
- Develop control procedures
- Develop contingency plans
- Develop preventive procedures
- Develop response procedures

**4. Monitor Risks:**
- Monitor risks continuously
- Monitor procedure effectiveness
- Monitor changes in risks
- Update evaluations

### Exploiting Opportunities (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Exploiting opportunities is essential to improve performance and success.

**Opportunity Exploitation Procedures:**

**1. Identify Opportunities:**
- Identify all potential opportunities
- Identify opportunity sources
- Identify opportunity impact
- Identify opportunity probability

**2. Evaluate Opportunities:**
- Evaluate probability of exploitation
- Evaluate severity of impact
- Determine opportunity level
- Determine priorities

**3. Exploit Opportunities:**
- Develop exploitation plans
- Provide required resources
- Execute plans
- Monitor execution

**4. Monitor Opportunities:**
- Monitor opportunities continuously
- Monitor exploitation effectiveness
- Monitor changes in opportunities
- Update evaluations

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What are risks
- What are opportunities
- Risk management
- Exploiting opportunities

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Risks and opportunities must be identified
- Risks and opportunities must be evaluated
- Risks must be managed
- Opportunities must be exploited

In the next lecture, we will discuss Quality Objectives.

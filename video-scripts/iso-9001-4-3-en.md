# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 4.3
## Quality Objectives
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss quality objectives. Defining objectives is essential for the success of the quality management system.

### What are Quality Objectives? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Quality objectives are specific objectives to improve product and service quality.

**Definition:**
- Measurable objectives
- Achievable objectives
- Time-bound objectives
- Responsibility-defined objectives

**Importance of Quality Objectives:**
- Provide clear direction
- Provide basis for measurement
- Provide incentive for improvement
- Provide basis for accountability

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Objectives must be SMART
- Objectives must be aligned with policy
- Objectives must be aligned with strategy
- Objectives must be communicated to all employees

### SMART Criteria (5:00 - 9:00)
**Speaker:** Good objectives must follow SMART criteria.

**SMART Criteria:**

**S - Specific:**
- Objectives must be clearly defined
- Objectives must be clear and understandable
- Objectives must not be vague

**M - Measurable:**
- Objectives must be measurable
- Objectives must have clear metrics
- Objectives must be trackable

**A - Achievable:**
- Objectives must be realistic
- Objectives must be achievable
- Objectives must be aligned with resources

**R - Relevant:**
- Objectives must be important
- Objectives must be aligned with policy
- Objectives must be aligned with strategy

**T - Time-bound:**
- Objectives must have an end date
- Objectives must be time-bound
- Objectives must have a timeline

### Types of Quality Objectives (9:00 - 13:00)
**Speaker:** There are several types of quality objectives.

**Main Types:**

**1. Product Quality Objectives:**
- Improve product quality
- Reduce defects
- Reduce complaints
- Increase customer satisfaction

**2. Process Quality Objectives:**
- Improve process efficiency
- Reduce waste
- Reduce time
- Reduce cost

**3. Service Quality Objectives:**
- Improve service quality
- Improve response speed
- Improve response accuracy
- Improve customer satisfaction

**4. Employee Quality Objectives:**
- Improve employee skills
- Improve employee training
- Improve employee satisfaction
- Reduce employee turnover

**5. Compliance Objectives:**
- Improve regulatory compliance
- Reduce violations
- Improve auditing
- Improve documentation

### Developing Quality Objectives (13:00 - 16:00)
**Speaker:** Developing quality objectives requires following specific procedures.

**Steps:**

**1. Analyze Current Situation:**
- Analyze current performance
- Analyze gaps
- Analyze needs
- Analyze opportunities

**2. Define Objectives:**
- Define objectives based on analysis
- Define objectives based on policy
- Define objectives based on strategy
- Define objectives based on resources

**3. Apply SMART Criteria:**
- Ensure objectives are specific
- Ensure objectives are measurable
- Ensure objectives are achievable
- Ensure objectives are relevant
- Ensure objectives are time-bound

**4. Approve Objectives:**
- Review objectives
- Approval from top management
- Documentation
- Signature

### Monitoring and Evaluating Objectives (16:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Monitoring and evaluating objectives is essential to ensure achievement.

**Procedures:**
- Monitor progress regularly
- Measure performance regularly
- Compare performance with objectives
- Identify deviations
- Address deviations
- Update objectives when necessary

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What are quality objectives
- SMART criteria
- Types of quality objectives
- Developing quality objectives
- Monitoring and evaluating objectives

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Objectives are essential for success
- Objectives must be SMART
- There are several types of objectives
- Continuous monitoring is essential

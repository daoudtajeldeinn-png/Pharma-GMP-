# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 2.3
## Quality Management System Scope
**Duration:** 15 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss the quality management system scope. Defining the scope is essential to correctly apply the standard.

### What is Quality Management System Scope? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** The quality management system scope defines what the system includes and what it does not include.

**Definition:**
- Internal and external boundaries and limits
- Products and services included
- Processes included
- Locations included

**Importance of Defining Scope:**
- Defines what the standard applies to
- Defines what the standard does not apply to
- Facilitates application
- Facilitates auditing

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Scope must be clear
- Scope must be realistic
- Scope must be documented
- Scope must be reviewed

### Elements of Defining Scope (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** Defining scope includes several elements.

**Main Elements:**

**1. Products and Services:**
- Products included
- Services included
- Products not included
- Services not included

**2. Processes:**
- Processes included
- Processes not included
- Critical processes
- Support processes

**3. Locations:**
- Locations included
- Locations not included
- Main locations
- Subsidiary locations

**4. Departments:**
- Departments included
- Departments not included
- Critical departments
- Support departments

### Exclusions (10:00 - 13:00)
**Speaker:** ISO 9001 allows certain exclusions.

**Permitted Exclusions:**
- Requirements that do not apply to the nature of the organization
- Requirements that do not apply to the system scope
- Requirements that do not affect the organization's ability to achieve compliance

**Conditions for Exclusions:**
- Must be justified
- Must be documented
- Must be approved
- Must not affect quality

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Exclusions must be limited
- Exclusions must be clear
- Exclusions must be reviewed
- Exclusions must be updated

### Documenting Scope (13:00 - 15:00)
**Speaker:** Documenting scope is essential for compliance.

**Content of Scope Document:**
- Description of products and services
- Description of processes
- Description of locations
- Description of justified exclusions
- Justification of exclusions
- Approval of scope

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Document must be clear
- Document must be current
- Document must be available
- Document must be traceable

### Summary (15:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What is quality management system scope
- Elements of defining scope
- Permitted exclusions
- Documenting scope

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Defining scope is essential
- Scope must be clear
- Exclusions are permitted under conditions
- Documentation is essential

In the next lecture, we will discuss Leadership.

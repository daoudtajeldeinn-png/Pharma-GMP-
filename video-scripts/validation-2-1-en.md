# Video Script: Validation - Unit 2.1
## Equipment Validation
**Duration:** 22 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss Equipment Validation. Equipment Validation is essential to ensure that equipment operates correctly and consistently.

### What is Equipment Validation? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Equipment Validation is the process of demonstrating that equipment operates correctly and achieves the intended purpose.

**Definition:**
- Demonstrating that equipment operates correctly
- Demonstrating that equipment produces consistent results
- Demonstrating that equipment complies with specifications
- Demonstrating that equipment is safe to use

**Importance of Equipment Validation:**
- Ensures product quality
- Reduces errors
- Ensures employee safety
- Essential for regulatory compliance

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Equipment Validation includes all types of equipment
- Equipment Validation must be performed before use
- Equipment Validation must be documented

### Equipment Validation Stages (5:00 - 12:00)
**Speaker:** Equipment Validation includes several organized stages.

**Stages:**

**1. DQ (Design Qualification):**
- Design qualification
- Review of technical specifications
- Review of materials used
- Ensure design meets requirements

**2. IQ (Installation Qualification):**
- Installation qualification
- Ensure correct installation
- Review drawings
- Verify connections

**3. OQ (Operational Qualification):**
- Operational qualification
- Test all functions
- Verify measurement accuracy
- Test safety systems

**4. PQ (Performance Qualification):**
- Performance qualification
- Test performance under normal operating conditions
- Verify consistency
- Test performance under extreme operating conditions

**Detailed Explanation for Each Stage:**

**DQ - Design Qualification:**
- Review technical specifications
- Ensure design meets requirements
- Review materials used
- Ensure ease of cleaning

**IQ - Installation Qualification:**
- Ensure correct installation
- Review drawings
- Verify connections
- Ensure regulatory compliance

**OQ - Operational Qualification:**
- Test all functions
- Verify measurement accuracy
- Test safety systems
- Ensure correct operation

**PQ - Performance Qualification:**
- Test performance under normal operating conditions
- Verify consistency
- Test performance under extreme operating conditions
- Ensure continuous quality

# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 3.3
## Roles and Responsibilities
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss roles and responsibilities in the quality management system. Defining roles and responsibilities is essential for the success of the system.

### Importance of Defining Roles and Responsibilities (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Defining roles and responsibilities is essential for several reasons.

**Main Reasons:**
1. **Clarity:** Ensures each person knows their role
2. **Responsibility:** Ensures each person is responsible for the quality of their work
3. **Accountability:** Facilitates accountability when problems arise
4. **Efficiency:** Increases efficiency and effectiveness
5. **Improvement:** Facilitates performance improvement

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Roles and responsibilities must be clear
- Roles and responsibilities must be documented
- Roles and responsibilities must be current
- Roles and responsibilities must be available

### Main Roles (5:00 - 12:00)
**Speaker:** There are several main roles in the quality management system.

**Role 1: Top Management**
**Responsibilities:**
- Define quality policy
- Provide resources
- Review quality management system
- Encourage continuous improvement
- Commitment to quality

**Role 2: Top Management Representative**
**Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate between top management and quality system
- Monitor quality system performance
- Report performance to top management
- Coordinate internal audit
- Coordinate management review

**Role 3: Quality Manager**
**Responsibilities:**
- Manage daily quality management system
- Develop quality procedures
- Monitor compliance
- Manage internal audit
- Manage continuous improvement

**Role 4: Department Managers**
**Responsibilities:**
- Implement quality management system in their departments
- Train employees on quality
- Monitor quality performance
- Address problems
- Improve processes

**Role 5: Employees**
**Responsibilities:**
- Follow quality procedures
- Participate in improvement
- Report problems
- Continuous training
- Commitment to quality

### Documenting Roles and Responsibilities (12:00 - 15:00)
**Speaker:** Documenting roles and responsibilities is essential for compliance.

**Content of Roles and Responsibilities Document:**
- Description of each role
- Description of responsibilities of each role
- Description of authorities of each role
- Description of relationships between roles
- Signature of approval

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Document must be clear
- Document must be current
- Document must be available
- Document must be traceable

### Reviewing and Updating Roles and Responsibilities (15:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Roles and responsibilities must be reviewed and updated regularly.

**When to Review Roles and Responsibilities?**
- During organizational changes
- During system changes
- During problems
- During improvements
- Periodically (annually)

**How to Review Roles and Responsibilities?**
- Evaluate effectiveness
- Evaluate clarity
- Evaluate appropriateness
- Identify improvement areas
- Update when necessary

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- Importance of defining roles and responsibilities
- Main roles
- Documenting roles and responsibilities
- Reviewing and updating roles and responsibilities

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Defining roles is essential
- Each person has a role and responsibilities
- Documentation is essential
- Continuous review is essential

In the next lecture, we will discuss Planning.

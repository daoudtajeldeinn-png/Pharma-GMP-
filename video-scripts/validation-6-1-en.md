# Video Script: Validation - Unit 6.1
## Common Validation Pitfalls
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss Common Validation Pitfalls. Knowing common mistakes is essential to avoid them and ensure validation success.

### Common Validation Mistakes (1:00 - 8:00)
**Speaker:** There are several common mistakes in validation that must be avoided.

**Main Mistakes:**

**1. Poor Planning:**
- Not defining scope clearly
- Not identifying required resources
- Not defining timeline
- Not defining criteria

**2. Incomplete Documentation:**
- Not documenting all activities
- Not documenting deviations
- Not documenting corrective actions
- Not documenting decisions

**3. Not Following Protocol:**
- Deviating from protocol without approval
- Not following defined steps
- Not recording data correctly
- Not addressing deviations

**4. Unclear Acceptance Criteria:**
- Not defining acceptance criteria clearly
- Unrealistic acceptance criteria
- Non-measurable acceptance criteria
- Non-compliant acceptance criteria

**5. Lack of Resources:**
- Shortage of trained staff
- Shortage of equipment
- Shortage of time
- Shortage of budget

### How to Avoid Mistakes (8:00 - 14:00)
**Speaker:** There are several ways to avoid common validation mistakes.

**Main Ways:**

**1. Good Planning:**
- Define scope clearly
- Identify required resources
- Define timeline
- Define criteria

**2. Complete Documentation:**
- Document all activities
- Document deviations
- Document corrective actions
- Document decisions

**3. Strict Protocol Adherence:**
- Follow protocol strictly
- Obtain approval before any deviation
- Record data correctly
- Address deviations immediately

**4. Clear Acceptance Criteria:**
- Define acceptance criteria clearly
- Realistic acceptance criteria
- Measurable acceptance criteria
- Compliant acceptance criteria

**5. Adequate Resources:**
- Provide trained staff
- Provide equipment
- Provide sufficient time
- Provide sufficient budget

### Common Mistake Examples (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Let's look at some examples of common mistakes.

**Example 1: Poor Planning:**
- Problem: Not defining scope clearly
- Result: Incomplete scope
- Solution: Define scope clearly before starting

**Example 2: Incomplete Documentation:**
- Problem: Not documenting deviations
- Result: Inability to trace
- Solution: Document all deviations immediately

**Example 3: Not Following Protocol:**
- Problem: Deviating from protocol without approval
- Result: Unacceptable results
- Solution: Obtain approval before any deviation

**Example 4: Unclear Acceptance Criteria:**
- Problem: Unrealistic acceptance criteria
- Result: Validation failure
- Solution: Define realistic acceptance criteria

**Example 5: Lack of Resources:**
- Problem: Shortage of trained staff
- Result: Validation delay
- Solution: Provide adequate resources

**Key Points to Remember:**

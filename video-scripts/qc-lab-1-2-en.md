### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss laboratory management. Effective management is essential for laboratory success.

**Speaker:** Laboratory management must follow specific principles.

**Basic Principles:**
1. **Leadership:** Effective leadership is essential
2. **Planning:** Comprehensive planning is essential
3. **Organization:** Good organization is essential
4. **Communication:** Effective communication is essential
5. **Improvement:** Continuous improvement is essential

- Management provides resources

- Register samples

- Store samples

**2. Analysis Department:**
- Perform analyses
- Use equipment
- Develop methods
- Monitor quality

**3. Quality Department:**
- Monitor quality
- Internal audit
- Document control
- CAPA management

**4. Administration Department:**
- Employee management
- Resource management
- Budget management
- Relationship management

**5. Maintenance Department:**
- Equipment maintenance
- Equipment calibration
- Equipment repair
- Equipment replacement

- Hire qualified employees
- Hire experienced employees
- Hire skilled employees
- Hire competent employees

**2. Training:**
- Training on analytical methods
- Training on equipment
- Training on quality management
- Training on safety

**3. Evaluation:**

- Evaluate competence regularly
- Evaluate skills regularly
- Evaluate behavior regularly

**4. Development:**
- Develop skills

### Equipment Management (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Equipment management is essential for laboratory success.

**Procedures:**

**1. Selection:**
- Select appropriate equipment
- Select accurate equipment
- Select reliable equipment
- Select maintainable equipment

**2. Installation:**
- Install equipment correctly
- Install equipment in appropriate location

- Install equipment according to regulations

**3. Maintenance:**
- Periodic equipment maintenance
- Preventive equipment maintenance
- Corrective equipment maintenance

**4. Calibration:**
- Periodic equipment calibration
- Calibration before use
- Calibration after repair
- Document all calibration

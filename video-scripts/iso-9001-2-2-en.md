# Video Script: ISO 9001 - Unit 2.2
## Stakeholders
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss stakeholders. Understanding stakeholders is essential for the success of the quality management system.

### Who are Stakeholders? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Stakeholders are individuals or organizations affected by the organization or affecting it.

**Definition:**
- Individuals or organizations affected
- Individuals or organizations affecting
- May be internal or external
- May be direct or indirect

**Importance of Understanding Stakeholders:**
- Helps identify needs
- Helps identify expectations
- Helps improve relationships
- Helps improve performance

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Stakeholders include customers
- Stakeholders include employees
- Stakeholders include suppliers
- Stakeholders include the community

### Types of Stakeholders (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** There are several types of stakeholders.

**Main Types:**

**1. Internal Stakeholders:**
- Employees
- Top management
- Shareholders
- Managers

**2. External Stakeholders:**
- Customers
- Suppliers
- Regulators
- Community

**3. Direct Stakeholders:**
- Direct customers
- Direct employees
- Direct suppliers

**4. Indirect Stakeholders:**
- Community
- Government
- Non-governmental organizations
- Competitors

### Stakeholder Needs and Expectations (10:00 - 14:00)
**Speaker:** Each stakeholder has different needs and expectations.

**Customer Needs:**
- Product quality
- Reasonable price
- Excellent service
- Product availability

**Employee Needs:**
- Safe work environment
- Fair compensation
- Development opportunities
- Work appreciation

**Supplier Needs:**
- On-time payment
- Long-term relationships
- Clear requirements
- Mutual collaboration

**Regulator Needs:**
- Regulatory compliance
- Regular reports
- Transparency
- Product quality

**Community Needs:**
- Environmental protection
- Social responsibility
- Safe products
- Community contribution

### Stakeholder Management (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Stakeholder management is essential for success.

**Procedures:**

**1. Identify Stakeholders:**
- Identify all stakeholders
- Identify key stakeholders
- Identify impact of each stakeholder
- Determine stakeholder priorities

**2. Understand Stakeholder Needs:**
- Collect information
- Analyze needs
- Analyze expectations
- Determine priorities

**3. Communicate with Stakeholders:**
- Continuous communication
- Clear communication
- Effective communication
- Regular communication

**4. Manage Stakeholder Expectations:**
- Define expectations clearly
- Manage expectations realistically
- Communicate changes
- Address problems immediately

**5. Review Relationships:**
- Review relationships regularly
- Identify improvement areas
- Develop improvements
- Evaluate effectiveness

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- Who are stakeholders
- Types of stakeholders
- Stakeholder needs and expectations
- Stakeholder management

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Stakeholders include several types
- Each stakeholder has different needs
- Understanding needs is essential
- Relationship management is essential

# Video Script: QC Lab & ISO/IEC 17025 - Unit 3.1
## Internal Audit
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss internal audit in the laboratory. Internal audit is essential to ensure compliance and quality.

### What is Internal Audit? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Internal audit is a systematic and independent examination of laboratory activities.

**Definition:**
- Systematic examination of activities
- Independent examination of activities
- Compliance evaluation
- Effectiveness evaluation

**Importance of Internal Audit:**
- Ensures compliance with standards
- Ensures quality of results
- Identifies improvement areas
- Essential for accreditation

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Internal audit must be comprehensive
- Internal audit must be objective
- Internal audit must be documented
- Internal audit must be followed up

### Internal Audit Procedures (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** Internal audit requires following specific procedures.

**Procedures:**

**1. Planning:**
- Define audit scope
- Define audit criteria
- Define audit schedule
- Define audit team

**2. Execution:**
- Execute audit according to plan
- Collect evidence
- Review documents
- Interview employees

**3. Reporting:**
- Prepare audit report
- Analyze results
- Identify deviations
- Define corrective actions

**4. Follow-up:**
- Follow up corrective actions
- Evaluate effectiveness
- Close audit
- Document

### Internal Audit Areas (10:00 - 14:00)
**Speaker:** Internal audit must cover all laboratory areas.

**Main Areas:**

**1. Quality Management:**
- Quality management system
- SOPs
- Document management
- CAPA management

**2. Personnel:**
- Training
- Competence
- Roles and responsibilities
- Performance

**3. Equipment:**
- Maintenance
- Calibration
- Validation
- Performance

**4. Analytical Methods:**
- Validation
- Proper application
- Accuracy
- Effectiveness

**5. Data:**
- Data management
- Data accuracy
- Data preservation
- Data traceability

### Follow-up Procedures (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Internal audit follow-up is essential to ensure effectiveness.

**Procedures:**

**1. Review Report:**
- Review audit report
- Review deviations
- Review corrective actions
- Approve report

**2. Execute Actions:**
- Execute corrective actions
- Monitor execution
- Address problems
- Document

**3. Evaluate Effectiveness:**
- Evaluate action effectiveness
- Evaluate improvement effectiveness
- Evaluate system effectiveness
- Identify improvement areas

**4. Close Audit:**
- Close audit if effective
- Re-audit if not effective
- Update records
- Document

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What is internal audit
- Internal audit procedures
- Internal audit areas
- Follow-up procedures

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Internal audit is essential
- Audit must be comprehensive
- Follow-up is essential for effectiveness
- Documentation is essential

# Video Script: QC Lab & ISO/IEC 17025 - Unit 3.3
## Accreditation
**Duration:** 18 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we will discuss laboratory accreditation. Accreditation is essential to ensure laboratory competence and reliability.

### What is Accreditation? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** Accreditation is the official recognition of laboratory competence.

**Definition:**
- Official recognition of laboratory competence
- Official recognition of laboratory's ability to produce accurate results
- Official recognition of laboratory's compliance with standards
- Essential for trust in results

**Importance of Accreditation:**
- Ensures laboratory competence
- Ensures result reliability
- Essential for trust
- Essential for working with clients

**Detailed Explanation:**
- Accreditation is granted by accredited bodies
- Accreditation requires comprehensive evaluation
- Accreditation requires periodic review
- Accreditation requires continuous improvement

### Accreditation Bodies (5:00 - 9:00)
**Speaker:** There are several accreditation bodies in different regions.

**Main Bodies:**

**1. ISO/IEC 17025:**
- International standard for laboratories
- Defines competence requirements
- Defines quality requirements
- Essential for international accreditation

**2. National Accreditation Bodies:**
- Accreditation bodies in each country
- Grant accreditation to laboratories
- Review laboratories periodically
- Monitor performance

**3. Regional Accreditation Bodies:**
- Regional accreditation bodies
- Grant accreditation to laboratories in the region
- Review laboratories periodically
- Monitor performance

**4. International Accreditation Bodies:**
- International accreditation bodies
- Grant accreditation to laboratories globally
- Review laboratories periodically
- Monitor performance

### Procedures for Obtaining Accreditation (9:00 - 14:00)
**Speaker:** Obtaining accreditation requires following specific procedures.

**Procedures:**

**1. Application:**
- Apply for accreditation
- Submit required documents
- Pay fees
- Define accreditation scope

**2. Initial Evaluation:**
- Review documents
- Review system
- Review procedures
- Identify improvement areas

**3. On-site Evaluation:**
- Visit laboratory
- Review facilities
- Review equipment
- Review personnel

**4. Testing:**
- Conduct proficiency tests
- Conduct calibration tests
- Conduct comparison tests
- Evaluate results

**5. Granting Accreditation:**
- Grant accreditation if laboratory is qualified
- Grant accreditation certificate
- Define accreditation scope
- Define accreditation period

### Maintaining Accreditation (14:00 - 18:00)
**Speaker:** Maintaining accreditation is essential for continuity.

**Procedures:**

**1. Periodic Review:**
- Periodic review by accreditation body
- Periodic review of system
- Periodic review of procedures
- Periodic review of performance

**2. Participation in External Testing:**
- Participate in proficiency tests
- Participate in comparison tests
- Evaluate performance
- Identify improvement areas

**3. Internal Audit:**
- Periodic internal audit
- Comprehensive system audit
- Procedure audit
- Performance audit

**4. Continuous Improvement:**
- Continuous system improvement
- Continuous procedure improvement
- Continuous performance improvement
- Continuous competence improvement

### Summary (18:00)
**Speaker:** In this lecture, we learned:
- What is accreditation
- Accreditation bodies
- Procedures for obtaining accreditation
- Maintaining accreditation

**Key Points to Remember:**
- Accreditation is essential for trust
- Obtaining accreditation requires effort
- Maintaining accreditation is essential
- Continuous improvement is essential

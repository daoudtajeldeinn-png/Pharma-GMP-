# Video Script: QC Lab & ISO/IEC 17025 - Unit 1.1
## Definition and Importance of QC Lab
**Duration:** 15 minutes

---

### Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
**Speaker:** Welcome to this lecture. Today we will discuss the definition of QC Lab and its importance in the pharmaceutical industry.

### What is QC Lab? (1:00 - 5:00)
**Speaker:** QC Lab is the quality control laboratory responsible for inspecting and testing materials and products.

**Definition:**
- Quality control laboratory
- Responsible for inspecting raw materials
- Responsible for inspecting final products
- Responsible for quality monitoring

**Main Functions:**
- Inspect raw materials
- Inspect products during manufacturing
- Inspect final products
- Monitor stability
- Microbiological testing

**Importance of QC Lab:**
- Ensures quality of raw materials
- Ensures quality of products
- Ensures patient safety
- Essential for regulatory compliance

### ISO/IEC 17025 (5:00 - 10:00)
**Speaker:** ISO/IEC 17025 is the international standard for laboratories.

**Definition:**
- International standard for laboratories
- Defines competence requirements
- Defines quality requirements
- Essential for accreditation

**Importance of ISO/IEC 17025:**
- Ensures laboratory competence
- Ensures quality of results
- Essential for accreditation
- Essential for trust

**Main Requirements:**
- Quality management
- Personnel
- Facilities and equipment
- Analytical methods
- Document management

### Role of QC Lab in Pharmaceutical Industry (10:00 - 15:00)
**Speaker:** QC Lab has a vital role in the pharmaceutical industry.

**Main Roles:**

**1. Raw Material Testing:**
- Test raw materials before use
- Ensure compliance with specifications
- Prevent use of unsuitable materials
- Ensure quality of final product

**2. In-Process Testing:**
- Test products during manufacturing
- Monitor process quality
- Prevent problem recurrence
- Ensure quality consistency

**3. Final Product Testing:**
- Test final products before release
- Ensure compliance with specifications
- Prevent release of unsuitable products
- Ensure product quality

**4. Stability Monitoring:**
- Monitor product stability
- Determine expiration date
- Ensure product effectiveness
- Ensure product safety

**5. Microbiological Testing:**
- Test for microbiological contamination
- Ensure product is free from bacteria
- Ensure product is free from fungi
- Ensure product safety

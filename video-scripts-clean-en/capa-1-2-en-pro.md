Understanding regulations is essential for compliance.
FDA CAPA Guidance (1:00 - 6:00) FDA CAPA Guidance is one of the most important regulatory references for CAPA.
Main Requirements: Effective CAPA System: Company must have an effective CAPA system Problem Investigation: All problems must be investigated Corrective Actions: Effective corrective actions must be developed Preventive Actions: Effective preventive actions must be developed Documentation: All CAPA activities must be documented FDA requires a comprehensive CAPA system FDA requires comprehensive investigations FDA requires effective actions FDA requires complete documentation ICH Q10 (6:00 - 10:00) ICH Q10 is an international guidance for pharmaceutical quality management system.
Main Requirements: Quality Management System: Company must have an effective quality management system Risk Management: Must include risk management Continuous Improvement: Must have continuous improvement Management Review: Must have periodic management review Communication: Must have effective communication ICH Q10 focuses on product lifecycle ICH Q10 focuses on risk management ICH Q10 focuses on continuous improvement ICH Q10 focuses on management review WHO GMP (10:00 - 14:00) WHO GMP provides guidelines for CAPA in the pharmaceutical industry.
